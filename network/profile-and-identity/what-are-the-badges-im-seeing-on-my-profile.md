# What are the badges I'm seeing on my profile

Badges are awarded to you by your Host! It means you have accomplished or achieved something that they want to acknowledge publicly.

Everyone in the Mighty Network will be able to see your Badges and you can also click on the Badge to learn more about it.

**Foundation Badges**

These recognise your initial steps and early commitments:

• Joining the program \
• Completing your initial assessment \
• Setting your first goals \
• Establishing your support network

**Learning Badges**

Awarded for completing educational content:

• Module completions \
• Video lessons watched \
• Worksheets finished \
• Key concepts mastered

**Practice Badges**

Given for applying what you've learned:

• Using coping strategies \
• Navigating triggers successfully \
• Implementing self-care routines \
• Managing urges effectively

**Milestone Badges**

Mark significant achievements in your journey:

• Days of sobriety \
• Program completion stages \
• Personal breakthroughs \
• Long-term consistency

**Community Badges**

Recognise your contributions to others:

• Supporting fellow members \
• Sharing your story \
• Offering encouragement \
• Embodying community values
