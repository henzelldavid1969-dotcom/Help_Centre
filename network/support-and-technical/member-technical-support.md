# Member Technical Support

Need Help? Here's How to Get the Right Support!

## Contact Your Host for Community-Related Queries

Your Host, the creator of your community, is your first point of contact for queries like:

* Refund requests.
* Promotions or discounts.
* Member or content issues.
* Access to Private or Secret Spaces.
* Becoming a Moderator or Co-Host.

### How to Reach Your Host

* Via Chat: Click on your Host's name in your Mighty Network to open their profile, then select "Chat".
* Other Methods: Check the community's pinned welcome posts for a post containing contact details like email or phone.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Decisions made by your Host are final and are not reversible by Mighty Networks.
{% endhint %}

## Technical Support from Mighty Networks' Customer Advocacy Team

For technical issues such as:

* Login help or password reset.
* Editing your profile or post permissions.
* Updating billing information.
* Leaving a Mighty Network or deleting your account.

## Quick Answers in Our Help Center

For general information, our Help Center is a great resource. Use the search function at the top-right of the page for instant answers.
