# Airplay & Chromecast

Chromecast and AirPlay let you stream Mighty Networks content from your device to a larger screen. This guide explains how to cast from the Mighty Networks mobile app and from the desktop web experience.

{% hint style="warning" %}
You will not be able to cast videos from an Android phone.
{% endhint %}

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Using Chromecast and AirPlay with the Mighty Networks App

* Make sure your mobile device and your Chromecast/AirPlay-compatible device are connected to the same Wi‑Fi network.
* Open the Mighty Networks app on your mobile device.
* Navigate to the content you want to stream.
* Tap the Cast or AirPlay icon within the content's playback controls.
* Choose the Chromecast or AirPlay device from the list that appears.
* Control playback from your mobile device while the content plays on the larger screen.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Using Chromecast and AirPlay with the Mighty Networks Desktop Account

* Confirm your computer and your Chromecast/AirPlay-compatible device are on the same Wi‑Fi network.
* Open your web browser and log in to your Mighty Networks account.
* Find the content you want to cast (video, webinar, etc.).
* Click the Chromecast or AirPlay icon in the media player to start casting.
* Select the target device from the list of available devices.
* Manage playback from your desktop while the content displays on the selected screen.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## Additional tips

* Keep your Chromecast or AirPlay-compatible device powered on and ready to receive the stream.
* Casting quality depends on your network speed and the content’s quality.
* Chromecast and AirPlay compatibility depends on your device’s casting capabilities; if your device doesn’t support casting, the option won’t be available.
