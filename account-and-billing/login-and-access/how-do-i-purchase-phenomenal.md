# How do I purchase Phenomenal?

### Choose your Phenomenal.

Phenomenal is available in three unique flavours:

- 
**Solo **- Work through the program online by yourself in your own time
​

- 
**Together** - Join a group of like-minded souls on one of my 4 x yearly cohorts
​

- 
**Private** - Work through the program with me as your coach and mentor.

Once you are sure which option you would like to follow, click the purchase button which will take you to the Phenomenal checkout.

**Note:** You can select your payment options on the checkout page and enter any discount codes you may have been provided.

### Accessing your Program.

Following checkout you will receive a confirmation email including your access link, username and password.

Simple, eh? If at any point you need assistance please reach out to support using Solly.
