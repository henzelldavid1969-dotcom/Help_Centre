# How much does Phenomenal cost?

Phenomenal offers three flexible options to suit your needs and lifestyle:

- 
**Solo Adventure: $399.00**

Self-paced program with live group coaching calls, community access, and comprehensive workbook. Perfect for independent learners who want flexibility with support.
​

- 
**Group Journey: $950.00**

Group-based experience with shared milestones, peer accountability, live sessions, and community connection. Ideal for those who thrive with others.
​

- 
**Fully Guided: $1250.00**

Fully personalised one-on-one program with dedicated coaching from David, customised pace, and individual attention. Complete privacy with maximum personal guidance.
​

Each option is designed to provide you with the support and tools you need for your sobriety journey. Remember, this is an investment in your future wellbeing and alcohol-free life.

Payment plans are available for the Group and Fully Guided programs.
