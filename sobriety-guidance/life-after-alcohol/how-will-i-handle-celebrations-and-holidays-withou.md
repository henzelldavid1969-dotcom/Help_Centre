# Sober Celebrations?

Handling work events, celebrations, and holidays sober can be challenging, but with some planning and strategies, you can navigate these situations successfully. Here are some tips:

## Tips for Staying Sober

### Make a Plan

Decide in advance how you'll handle offers of alcohol and what you'll say if someone asks why you're not drinking.

### Avoid Triggers

Identify situations or people that might tempt you to drink and plan to avoid or manage them.

### Bring a Sober Buddy

Having a friend who supports your decision can provide moral support and help you stay accountable.

### Keep a Non-Alcoholic Drink in Hand

This can help you feel more comfortable and avoid questions about why you're not drinking.

### Create New Traditions

Focus on activities that don't revolve around alcohol, like games, sports, or creative projects.

### Practice Self-Care

Ensure you're taking care of your physical and mental health, which can help you stay strong in social situations.

### Set Boundaries

Be ready to say no to events that might be too challenging and prioritize your well-being.

By preparing in advance and focusing on your goals, you can enjoy these occasions without alcohol. Remember, it's about celebrating your achievements and enjoying the company of others in a way that aligns with your values.
