# Other Drinkers.

Maintaining sobriety while living with or spending time with drinkers requires clear strategies and strong boundaries:

## Understanding the Challenge:



**Common Situations:**

* Partner drinks at home
* Family gatherings with alcohol
* Shared social circles
* Holiday celebrations
* Regular routines involving alcohol ​



**Potential Complications:**

* Triggers in your living space
* Relationship dynamics
* Social pressure
* Emotional challenges
* Lifestyle differences

## Creating a Supportive Environment:



**Physical Space:**

* Designated alcohol-free areas
* Separate storage for drinks
* Your own beverage space
* Clear visual boundaries
* Safe retreat spaces ​
* Communication Strategies

### With Partner:

* Express your needs clearly
* Share your triggers
* Discuss compromises
* Set boundaries
* Plan together

### With Family:

* Communicate your decision
* Request support
* Share your boundaries
* Explain your needs
* Stay firm but kind

### Personal Boundaries:

* What you're comfortable with
* Your limits and triggers
* Your non-negotiables
* Your support needs
* Your safe spaces

### Household Boundaries:

* Alcohol storage
* Drinking areas
* Social gatherings
* Shared activities
* Recovery support

### Practical Solutions:



**For Home Life:**

* Create alcohol-free zones
* Stock non-alcoholic alternatives
* Develop new shared rituals
* Plan sober activities
* Establish quiet spaces ​



**For Social Situations:**

* Arrive early, leave early
* Bring your own drinks
* Have an exit strategy
* Plan alternative activities
* Stay connected with support system ​



**For Emotional Support:**

* Build external support network
* Attend support groups
* Maintain personal practices
* Seek counselling if needed
* Connect with a ober community

### Managing Relationship Dynamics:



**With Partner:**

* Regular check-ins
* Shared understanding
* Mutual respect
* Open communication
* Collaborative problem-solving ​



**With Family:**

* Clear expectations
* Consistent boundaries
* Respectful dialogue
* Alternative traditions
* New ways to connect

### Remember:

* Your sobriety comes first
* Others' choices are their own
* You deserve support and understanding
* Boundaries are healthy and necessary
* Change is possible while maintaining relationships
