# Should I just switch to cannabis?

This is a question I get a lot. However, switching from alcohol to cannabis is not necessarily a straightforward solution and may not address the underlying issues related to alcohol consumption.

Here are some considerations that come to mind:

#### Alcohol Vs Cannabis

* **Health Risks**: Both alcohol and cannabis have their own health risks. While alcohol can lead to liver damage, addiction, and various health issues, cannabis can also have negative effects, particularly on mental health and cognitive function. ​
* **Addiction Potential**: Cannabis can be addictive for some individuals, and using it as a substitute for alcohol may lead to a different form of dependence. ​
* **Mental Health**: Cannabis use can exacerbate anxiety and depression in some people, which may not be beneficial if you are already struggling with these issues.

#### Suggestions

* **Consult a Professional**: It is advisable to speak with a healthcare professional or a counsellor who specialises in substance use. They can help you explore healthier coping mechanisms and provide support tailored to your situation. ​
* **Consider Therapy**: Engaging in therapy or support groups can be beneficial in addressing the reasons behind excessive drinking and finding healthier alternatives. ​
* **Moderation and Mindfulness**: If you choose to use cannabis, consider doing so mindfully and in moderation, just as you would with alcohol.
