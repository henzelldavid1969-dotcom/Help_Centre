# I've tried to stop several times before. What makes Phenomenal any different?

Quitting alcohol can be challenging, but Phenomenal employs several strategies that might make this attempt more successful:

## Key Strategies for Quitting Alcohol

- 
**Know Your Why**: Clearly understanding your reasons for quitting can provide motivation and focus.
​

- 
Reflect on the benefits you expect, such as improved health or better relationships.
​

- 
**Set Clear Goals**: Define what success looks like for you, whether it's complete abstinence or reducing intake.
​

- 
Break down your goals into manageable steps.
​

- 
**Identify Triggers**: Recognise situations or emotions that lead you to drink and plan how to handle them.
​

- 
This might involve avoiding certain places or finding new coping mechanisms.
​

- 
**Build a Support System**: Surround yourself with friends, family, or support groups who understand and support your decision.
​

- 
**Plan for Cravings**: Have strategies in place for when cravings hit, such as engaging in a hobby or calling a friend.
​

- 
**Remove Access to Alcohol**: Make it harder to drink by not keeping alcohol at home.
​

- 
**Consult Professionals**: Seek advice from healthcare providers who can offer guidance and support.

By focusing on these strategies, you can create a supportive environment and mindset that may help you succeed this time. Remember, it's a journey, and seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.
