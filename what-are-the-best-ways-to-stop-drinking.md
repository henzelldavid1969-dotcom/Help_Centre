# What are the best ways to stop drinking?

Stopping drinking can be a challenging journey, but there are several effective strategies and resources that can help you succeed. Here are some of the best ways to stop drinking:

## Key Strategies

### 1. Set Clear Goals

- 
Decide whether you want to cut back or quit entirely.

- 
Set specific, achievable goals and track your progress.
​

### 2. Seek Professional Help

- 
Consult with healthcare professionals who can provide medical advice and treatment options.

- 
Consider medications that can help reduce cravings and withdrawal symptoms.

### 3. Join Support Groups or Programs

- 
Connect with support groups like Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) for community support and shared experiences.

- 
Consider something like Phenomenal if AA sounds a bit scary
​

### 4. Behavioural Therapies

- 
Engage in therapies that address the psychological aspects of addiction, such as cognitive-behavioural therapy (CBT).

### 5. Develop Coping Strategies

- 
Identify triggers and develop strategies to manage cravings and avoid situations that encourage drinking.

### 6. Build a Support Network

- 
Surround yourself with supportive friends and family who understand your goals.

### 7. Adopt a Healthy Lifestyle

- 
Focus on nutrition, exercise, and stress management to improve your overall well-being.
