# Contact & Support

Phenomenal Sobriety exists because recovery is possible, meaningful, and worth pursuing. These guidelines exist not to restrict you, but to create a space where genuine transformation can happen.

We're building something special here - a community that combines professional support with peer connection, evidence-based approaches with meaning-making, and accountability with compassion.

### Contact & Support

**For Questions About Guidelines:**

* Message @David (Community Founder)
* Email: hello@phenomenalsobriety.com
* Use the Help/Support section in the community

**For Reporting Violations:**

* Use the Report feature on any post
* Message moderators directly
* Email urgent concerns to hello@phenomenalsobriety.com

**For Technical Support:**

* Check the FAQ section
* Message @Solly (AI companion)
* Contact hello@phenomenalsobriety.com

**For Crisis Support:**

* Emergency: 911 (US)
* Emergency: 999 (UK)
* National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
* Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
* SAMHSA Helpline: 1-800-662-4357
