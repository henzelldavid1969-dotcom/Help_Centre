# Using Solly

Our AI companion, Solly, is here to support your journey. When interacting with Solly:

**Remember:**

* Solly is a tool, not a replacement for human connection or therapy
* Conversations with Solly are private but should still follow community standards
* Solly can provide support, but cannot handle crisis situations
* Use Solly for reflection, accountability, and accessing resources

**Best Practices:**

* Be honest about your feelings and challenges
* Use Solly for journaling, goal-setting, and processing emotions
* Ask questions about course materials and recovery concepts
* Report any concerning or unhelpful responses to staff
