# Privacy & Confidentiality

**What You Should Do:**

* Keep all member stories, struggles, and personal information confidential
* Use first names only when referencing others
* Get explicit permission before sharing someone's story elsewhere
* Respect that what's shared here is not for public consumption

**What We Do:**

* We will never share your personal information without your consent
* Your participation is between you and this community
* We do not sell or share member data
* All course content and member discussions are private

**Exception:** If we believe someone is at imminent risk of harm to themselves or others, we may need to take appropriate action, including contacting authorities.

#### Content Ownership

* You retain ownership of your posts and contributions
* By posting, you grant Phenomenal Sobriety a license to display your content within the community
* Course materials, AI companion interactions, and program content are proprietary
* Do not copy, reproduce, or distribute program materials without permission
