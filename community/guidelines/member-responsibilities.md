# Member Responsibilities

### Your Responsibilities as a Member

#### Active Participation

* Engage regularly with course materials
* Contribute to discussions when you can
* Support other members in their journeys
* Provide feedback to help us improve

#### Personal Boundaries

* Know your limits and honour them
* Step back when discussions become overwhelming
* Mute or leave conversations that aren't serving your recovery
* Communicate your needs clearly

#### Accountability

* Be honest about your progress and challenges
* Follow through on commitments you make
* Take responsibility for your actions and words
* Ask for help when you need it

#### Growth Mindset

* Approach recovery as a learning journey
* Be open to new perspectives and approaches
* Challenge yourself while practicing self-compassion
* Celebrate progress, not just perfection
