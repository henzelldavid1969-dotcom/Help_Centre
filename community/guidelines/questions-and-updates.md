# Questions & Updates

#### Questions About These Guidelines?

If you're unsure whether something is appropriate:

* Ask a moderator before posting
* Err on the side of caution
* Consider whether it serves the community's wellbeing
* Think about how you'd want to be treated

#### Updates to Guidelines

We may update these guidelines as our community evolves. When we do:

* We'll notify members of significant changes
* We'll explain why changes were made
* We'll provide a grace period for adjustment
* We'll remain open to member feedback

#### Feedback Welcome

Your input helps us improve:

* Share suggestions for guideline clarifications
* Report concerns about community culture
* Offer ideas for better supporting members
* Help us build the community you need
